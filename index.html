My Philosophy

  • Leading from the front.

    While not always possible, I prefer to dive in when I can and be involved in the work, working left of my developer/engineer peers.

  • Being technical.

    There is a lot of success you can find with your SMEs with the right soft skills, but it's important to be open-minded and willing to learn. It's also difficult to provide direction when you're not up to speed on the basics, which can cause chaos and sign you and your teams up for difficult tasks.

  • People first.

    The best way to get the best out of people is by allowing them to flourish. How to do this can widely vary from team to team, or even from person to person. Also, replacing personnel can be extremely costly, from the time it takes to recruit, to teaching the necessary technical skills, and developing the necessary organizational knowledge.

  • Agility

    Agility and Adaptability is important for individuals to rise up and meet new challenges, whether it's building a new skill or new relationships. It's also important to teams to ensure peak performance and maximum innovation.
